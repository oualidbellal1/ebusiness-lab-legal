Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to E Business Lab. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users and their customers. This Privacy Policy clarifies how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our TikTok messaging automation service.

2. Data Accessed from TikTok

In order to provide our service, E Business Lab requires access to specific information from your TikTok account. The application only accesses the messages necessary to generate automated responses. We explicitly minimize our data access scope to only what is strictly required to read incoming inquiries and formulate appropriate replies.

3. How the Data is Used

The information we access is used exclusively for operating the service. Specifically, message content is read and processed through our system—which is connected to n8n automation workflows—to assist your customers and intelligently answer common questions. We do not use this data for profiling, marketing, or any purpose outside of the automated customer assistance workflow.

4. Data Storage and Protection

We do not retain message data longer than is strictly necessary to generate a response and ensure the system is functioning correctly. Data processed through our workflows is handled transiently where possible, and any logs maintained for error reporting or service improvement are pseudonymized and tightly secured.

5. Third-Party Sharing Statement

We maintain a strict policy regarding the sharing of your data. No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties. Data is only transmitted securely between TikTok APIs, our application, and your designated n8n automation workflows for processing purposes.

6. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect chat data against unauthorized access, destruction, loss, alteration, or misuse. Our systems utilize modern encryption protocols for data in transit and adhere to industry best practices in secure software development.

7. Changes to the Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
